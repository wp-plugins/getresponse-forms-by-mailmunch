== Description ==

> GetResponse Forms by MailMunch allows you to painlessly add GetResponse sign up forms to your WordPress site. The form captures the lead and sends it to specific GetResponse lists automatically. You can set it to no op-in, opt-in, or double opt-in. You can add forms to posts, pages or sidebar, and also open it as a popup or top bar. Simply sign up and connect your GetResponse account into the plugin admin settings and it will pull in all your GetResponse lists. From there you can choose the lists you want to make forms for.

== Installation ==

This section describes how to install the plugin and get started using it.

= Version 2.8+ =
1. Unzip our archive and upload the entire `getresponse-mailmunch` directory to your `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Look for "GetResponse" in the WordPress admin menu and click it
4. Sign up and create your first optin form
5. Choose the optin form type: Popover, Embedded or Top Bar
6. Connect GetResponse and choose a list
7. Start collecting user emails and growing your subscriber list!

= Advanced =
If you are using Wordpress v3.0 or higher, you can use the short-code given during the optin form creation. It will look something like this:
` [mailmunch-XYZ] `

= Do I need to have a GetResponse Account? =
Yes, you can register for one for free at [GetResponse](https://secure.getresponse.com/create_trial/ "GetResponse Signup").

= What do I do first? =
The first step is to activate the plugin and sign up. Then we will guide you through the process of connecting your GetResponse account and choosing the list. This is done on the WordPress admin "GetResponse" menu page.

= Do I need GetResponse's API Key? =
You don't have to worry about that. We will guide you through the process.

= How do I add my GetResponse lists? =
After you connect your GetResponse account, the plugin will fetch all of your GetResponse list information and load it into the plugin admin. You can simply choose the lists you want to make forms for.

= How do I add the GetResponse sign up forms to my site? =
We offer a variety of forms: Popups, Embedded, Top Bar, etc. You can choose which pages, posts or categories to show it on. You can also use a shortcode to add a form to a specific area in a page or post. Everything is done visually.

= Does this plugin slow down my site? =
Absolutely not. GetResponse by MailMunch is designed with high performance and scalability in mind. It's completely asynchronous and hosted on Amazon's highly scalable infrastructure. Some of our customers have very high traffic websites.
